Titanium Divison: Nuking the Farm

Problem Statement
Bessie the cow is invading the neighboring farm, which can be represented as a `N\times M` grid! At the nuke store, there are `K` types of nukes, and the `i`th nuke has a power of `p_i` and a cost of `c_i.` This means that its blast radius will be `p_i.`

Bessie wants to nuke the entire farm, so that Farmer Nhoj will be unable to escape. Help Bessie find the minimum cost to nuke the entire farm. (One type of nuke can be purchased multiple times.)
Input
The first line of input will contain three integers: `N`, `M`, and `K.`
The next `K` lines will contain numbers `p_i` and `c_i`, the power and cost of each nuke, respectively.
Output
Your output will consist on one integer: the least cost of the nukes that Bessie needs to nuke the entire farm.
