Titanium Divison: Nuking the Farm

Problem Statement
Bessie the cow is invading the neighboring farm, which can be represented as a `N\times M` grid! Last time, the nukes she used failed to detonate, so she is now resorting to arson!
After Bessie sets the fires, `K` time will pass before Farmer Nhoj wakes up and puts out the fire. Bessie wants to burn down the entire farm within `K` units of time.
She has two types of fires: slow fire and fast fire.
A single square of fast fire will turn into
**#**
*###*
#####
*###*
**#**
in one unit of time, where # represents burned area
A single square of slow fire will turn into
*****
**#**
*###*
**#**
*****
in one unit of time.
Setting one square of fast fire takes two Moonies, and setting a square of slow fire takes one Moony.
Find the least cost it will take for Bessie to burn down the farm!
Input
Input will consist of one line containing `N,` `M,` and `K.`
Output
Your output will consist on one integer: the least cost of the fires that Bessie needs light to burn down the entire farm.
