Dirt Divison: Cowculus


Problem Statement

Bessie the cow is taking calculus lessons at the Acowdemia! She is currently learning to compute derivatives and integrals of polynomials! Every day, she has `N` `(1\le N\le10^6)` homework problems. Because Bessie is , she needs your help with solving the homework problems.

Input

Your input consists of a single integer `N`, followed by the `N` problems
Each problem has two lines. The first line has a single integer: `K.` `K` is the degree of the polynomial.

Output

Your output will consist of `N` lines.
On each line, you will output a single integer `A,` the sum of the coefficients of the derivative for that problem.
